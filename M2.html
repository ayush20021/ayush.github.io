    THE ALL-NEW BMW M2 COUPÉ– PRODUCT HIGHLIGHTS.

    Driving dynamics and design features of The All-New BMW M2 Coupé.

    High-Performance M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder petrol engine.

    A top athlete that offers pure driving pleasure: The M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder petrol engine features dynamic power delivery and the characteristic throaty M engine sound. Highly efficient technologies such as double VANOS, Valvetronic, high precision injection and a TwinScroll turbocharger provide excellent output.

    Manual transmission(Optional).

    The manual transmission with six gears promises pure driving pleasure. Selecting the gears yourself and manually engaging them compared to the automatic transmission, allows you to experience a direct, true connection to the drive of your BMW – and therefore sporty driving in its purest form. You also benefit from the weight-optimised transmission design.

    Adaptive M suspension.

    With the adaptive M chassis with variable shock absorber adjustment, the characteristics of the chassis can be individually adjusted – from comfortable to particularly sporty with pronounced agile handling. With Driving Experience Control, you can adapt the electronically controlled chassis quickly and easily to the road conditions and driving situation.
